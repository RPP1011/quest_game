- **Streamlit viz** at `tools/viz_labels.py` for browsing labels

### Finetune experiments (one path tried, one ruled out)

| version | approach | overall r vs Claude | parse failures | verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| absolute v2 | per-dim row, prompt-loss | 0.05 | 5% | overfit, dim-blind |
| absolute v3 | batched, completion-only | 0.42 | 0% | shipping baseline |
| pairwise | A/B preference | 53% acc (chance=50%) | n/a | dead end on 1.2B |

**Conclusion:** LFM1.2B can do absolute-score distillation with batched
output (sensory_density r=0.72 even). Pairwise literary comparison
exceeds the model's capacity; would need 9B+ or a different architecture.

   `tools/finetune/build_arc_dataset.py` builds pairwise from them.
   Wait until passage-scale finetune patterns are validated before
   committing GPU time.

### Medium-term (when retrieval lands)

7. **Cross-quest retrieval** — currently per-quest only. Useful for

8. **Voice retriever heavy variant** — LLM post-pass to attribute
   utterances to speakers, enabling per-character voice continuity.
9. **Continuous evaluation** — automate the A/B eval so each retrieval
